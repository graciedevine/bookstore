If I'm ever stranded, let it be in a bookstore!

We are a used paperback bookstore who trades and sells used papaerback books. Paperbacks Ink has been part of the Hampton Roads community since 1982.

Don't just come in to browse our selection of over 50,000 titles that turn over daily. We also proudly brew teas from Discover Tea and coffee from Norfolk Coffee & Tea Co. Every month we have different themed drinks, as well as craft nights and book signings with local authors.

Trade-in books get 25% of cover price - in store credit towards purchase of more books.

Used books sell for 50% of cover price.
